Bounty Exchange

Gone are the days when bounty hunters used to get hundreds or even thousands of dollars doing what may seem like mundane tasks for weeks and weeks at an end. Now-a-days one would be lucky to find a good project that pays bounty hunters well for their hard work. Sometimes after working so hard for months, promoting a project, these hard working people find themselves receiving a very small share of the bounty tokens worth $5 - $20.

They are then faced with the challenge of liquidating these tokens so they can buy necessities for their families. They find themselves in one of three scenarios:

  1. They can't send their tokens to an exchange so they can trade them for more liquid coins like Bitcoin, why, because the said exchange has a minimum deposit and they sadly can't meet it.
  2. They are able to send the tokens to the exchange, but they are rudely awakened to the reality that they can't trade their tokens because they don't have enough to meet the trading minimums.
  3. If they are lucky enough to trade the tokens, they only end up with very little amount to spend because withdrawal fees of some exchanges are just outrageous to say the least.

Loonie Freelancers

Despite the fact that there are dozens of freelancing websites out there, Upwork, Freelancer.com, fivver.com, to name just a few, there are still millions of Freelancers who are not getting an opportunity to showcase their skills.

It is so hard for newcomers to get accepted on some of these platforms, once accepted, it's near impossible to get their first paying clients no matter how good a freelancer is.

Our platform will give every freelancer a fair opportunity to prove themselves and will give a fair chance to everyone for getting a good rating by holding anonymous competitions in different categories and holding blind voting.

Loonie Dex

Decentralized exchanges give users a piece of mind knowing that their funds are not held in a centralized server somewhere where they can be easily targeted by hackers. However, most dexes have the downside of being very complicated to use. Our exchange will bring the masses to decentralized exchanges by offering a fast and user-friendly platform which utelizes two parallel blockchains that are synchronized by relays .

Another way is to day trade, taking advantage of the volatility of the crypto markets the day trader buys and sells frequently using some form of analysis tools. This is the most risky and it is not recommended especially to beginners as you can quickly lose your capital.

The Loonie platform will have three services and the holders of LNI can use the token to pay fees for the services offered on the platform. These include, Loonie Bounty Exchange, Loonie Freelance, and Loonie Dex. On the bounty exchange, users will be able to convert multiple tokens of any size to LNI and the exchange the LNI conviniently for whatever token or coin they want. On the Freelancing platform, the holders will be able to use the token to promote their bids and to pay for certifications and for those that are posting jobs, to use the token to pay the fees for using the platform. On the dex, holders of Loonie will pay no fees for trading their tokens.

This is the platform for bounty hunters to consolidate their many small value tokens so they can easily liquidate only one token, either the LNI or any token of their choosing that will be listed on the platform. The exchange will use smart contracts and users will be sending their funds to the smart contract after escrow where they will be held until the other party sends the tokens that are being exchanged. Upon receipt of the tokens from both parties, the smart contract will then release them to the respective receipient.

There are dozens of Freelancing platforms and they have so many rules and conditions for users of their platforms and Loonie Freelance will also have rules and regulations but we will be different in that the token holders will be able to contribute to the governing rules through suggestions and voting. Our aim will not be to please everyone, but to be as fair as possible to every party involved.
